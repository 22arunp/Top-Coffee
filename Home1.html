About Us

As a climate positive workforce, every member of The Eco Coffee Company team will help plant around 19 trees each, per month. Sequestering around 12 tonnes of CO2, enough to negate their annual commuting miles (roughly 18,000 miles per employee). Click here to keep track of what we've achieved so far. This is on top of what our customers can help with, for only 50p you can plant a tree to make your next order climate positive. We aim to have helped plant 4,000 Trees in developing communities , globally, by January 2023... And while offsetting carbon emissions is a positive thing, we here at The Eco Coffee Company know that it is merely a drop in the ocean of what is needed to help maintain, grow and regenerate the planet. So far we have helped plant.... 3,727 Trees Planted

Our Products

Locally roasted to us in Dorset, Eco Coffee is a selection of coffee beans and ground coffee, which once used we recycle the spent coffee grounds. The spent coffee grounds are dried and made into ‘Eco Coffee Logs’, which can be used in wood burning stoves or domestic fire places. Or, they can be recycled and used as fuel pellets to roast the next batch of Eco Coffee, so completing the zero-waste loop. All our coffee bags are commercially recyclable using a zero-waste collection with TerraCycle™. Simply select your blend below and then your preferred size and style.
